Brisbane Outdoors — Support

We value your experience and are dedicated to helping you get the most out of Brisbane Outdoors. If you're facing any difficulties or experiencing any issues, we're here to help!

Please don't hesitate to reach out directly to our developer, Sebastian Bolling, who is always ready to assist you. You can reach him at sebastian.bolling@gmail.com.

A few quick answers:

— Park data (BBQs, toilets, playgrounds, drinking fountains, dog off-leash areas) comes from Brisbane City Council's open data and OpenStreetMap. If something at a park is broken or missing in real life, the fastest fix is the "Report a fault to Council" link on the park's detail page in the app.

— If a playground looks wrong or missing, note that playground locations are community-maintained OpenStreetMap data — tell us and we'll look into it.

— Purchases: the one-time unlock is tied to your Apple Account. If you reinstall or switch devices, tap "Restore Purchases" on the unlock screen and it comes back at no charge.

Whether you have a technical question, a suggestion for improvement, or any other concerns, your feedback is always appreciated.

Thank you for choosing Brisbane Outdoors!
