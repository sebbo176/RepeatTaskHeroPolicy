Sydney & Melbourne Outdoors — Support

We value your experience and are dedicated to helping you get the most out of Sydney & Melbourne Outdoors. If you're facing any difficulties or experiencing any issues, we're here to help!

Please don't hesitate to reach out directly to our developer, Sebastian Bolling, who is always ready to assist you. You can reach him at sebastian.bolling@gmail.com.

A few quick answers:

— Two cities, one app: tap the city name at the top of the map to switch between Sydney and Melbourne. Each city's parks are bundled in the app and work fully offline.

— Park data (BBQs, toilets, playgrounds, drinking water, and dog off-leash areas in Sydney) comes from OpenStreetMap together with council and state open data. Because much of it is community-maintained, an occasional amenity may be missing or out of date — let us know and we'll look into it.

— Purchases: the one-time unlock covers both cities and is tied to your Apple Account. If you reinstall or switch devices, tap "Restore Purchases" on the unlock screen and it comes back at no charge.

Whether you have a technical question, a suggestion for improvement, or any other concerns, your feedback is always appreciated.

Thank you for choosing Sydney & Melbourne Outdoors!
